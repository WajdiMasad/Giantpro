The Beginning

One Blimp Started It All

Over 30 years ago, in 1992, Giant Promotions was born with the purchase of just one inflatable blimp. A family-owned and proudly Canadian company, Giant has grown from its humble beginning as a home-based business to a strong contender in the Halifax marketplace and beyond — servicing all of Atlantic Canada.

As the company grew, employees were hired, and a new warehouse was constructed in 2013, allowing us to better service existing clients and have room for future growth.

Community

Giving Back to Atlantic Canada

We proudly support our community, contributing to Adsum House, the SPCA, and Metro Turning Point, as well as assisting our core group of charities at various events throughout the year.

Our team prides themselves on the level of service and innovation we bring to our clients — always looking for new and exciting products to help them drive their business by drawing attention and increasing traffic.

We look forward to continuing to grow our business, hiring more local employees, and expanding our selection of products and services while helping our clients grow theirs.
