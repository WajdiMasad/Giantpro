Our Story

Family-Owned.
Proudly Canadian.

In 1992, Giant Promotions was born with the purchase of just one inflatable blimp. What started as a home-based business has grown into a powerhouse servicing all of Atlantic Canada — with a dedicated warehouse built in 2013 and a team that treats every event like family.

We proudly support our community, contributing to Adsum House, the SPCA, and Metro Turning Point, while constantly innovating to bring fresh, attention-grabbing products to our clients.
