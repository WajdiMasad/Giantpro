Our Story

Family-Owned.
Proudly Canadian.

In 1992, Giant Promotions was born with the purchase of just one inflatable blimp. What started as a home-based business has grown into a powerhouse servicing all of Atlantic Canada — with a dedicated warehouse built in 2013 and a team that treats every event like family.

We proudly support our community, contributing to Adsum House, the SPCA, and Metro Turning Point, while constantly innovating to bring fresh, attention-grabbing products to our clients.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve in Atlantic Canada?

Giant Promotions serves all of Atlantic Canada including Halifax, Dartmouth, and the entire Halifax Regional Municipality (HRM), as well as Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, and Newfoundland. We deliver and set up directly at your venue.

How do I rent a bounce house or inflatable in Halifax?

Browse our product catalog, add items to your quote list, and submit a free quote request. Our team will respond within 24 hours with pricing and availability. We handle delivery, setup, and pickup.

Do you offer casino night packages?

Yes! We offer complete casino night packages including blackjack tables, roulette, poker, slot machines, and all accessories. We can also provide trained dealers for your event.

What types of events do you supply equipment for?

We supply equipment for corporate events, weddings, festivals, fundraisers, school events, grand openings, holiday celebrations, community events, and private parties. 300+ products for events of any size.

Do you offer LED sign and digital billboard rentals?

Absolutely. We offer mobile LED billboard trucks, portable LED signs, and indoor/outdoor digital displays for advertising and events across Nova Scotia.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance. For popular items during peak season (May–September) or holidays, booking 4–8 weeks ahead is recommended. Call 902-456-6487 to check availability.
